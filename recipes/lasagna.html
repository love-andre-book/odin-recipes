Lasagna with pecorino cheese

Description

This formerly secret recipe has been passed down in generations and was given to me by my italian mother on my 16th birthday. On my 30th birthday, my mother agreed that this glorious lasagna (that will be the result of perfectly executing the steps below) is simply too good to be kept a sectret. And that is why I have decided to share it with you all.

Ingredients: Sauce

  • 500g ground beef
  • 100g butter
  • 100g finely grated pecorino cheese
  • 400g tomato sauce (preferably home made with napolitan tomatoes)
  • 12 lasagne plates
  • 2 medium sized onion
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 bouillon cube
  • pepper and salt

Ingredients: Bechamel Sauce

  • pepper and salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/3 cup of flour
  • 100g parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Turn the oven on at 350 degrees (175 celsius).
  2. Grab a large and deep grying pan and melt half of the butter while chopping up the onions.
  3. Put the onions and beef in the frying pan and fry until most of the red color is gone.
  4. Put the tomato sauce, bouillon and spices in the pan and let it boil for a couple of minutes, stir regularly.
  5. Prepare the bechamel sauce by carefully pouring the flour in boiling milk in a small sauce pan while stirring intensly.
  6. Pour down the grated parmesan cheese and let it melt, season with some pepper and salt.
  7. Pour the pecorino in the meat sauce and stir for a couple of minutes on low heat.
  8. Grab a large oven dish and start with layering the lasagna starting with the meat sauce.
  9. The final layer should be topped with bechamel sauce and some more parmesan.
  10. Put in oven for 40 minutes, then let cool for 5 more minutes.
